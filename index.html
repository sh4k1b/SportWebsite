About the Cricket World Cup 2025

The Cricket World Cup 2025 brings together the best cricketing nations to compete for the ultimate prize in the sport. Hosted in the United Kingdom, the tournament will feature thrilling matches, passionate fans, and unforgettable moments.

From legendary rivalries to the rise of new stars, this event promises to be a celebration of cricket's rich history and exciting future. Whether your at the stadium or watching from home, get ready for a month of world-class cricket!
